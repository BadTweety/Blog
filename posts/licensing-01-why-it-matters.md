Software licensing sounds like paperwork until it costs you a product launch. This series walks through the essentials, one short post at a time.

## Why bother

- Using a library means accepting its license — whether you read it or not.
- "Free to download" and "free to use commercially" are **not** the same thing.
- One GPL dependency in the wrong place can obligate you to open-source your code.

```text
dependency → license → obligations → your product
```
